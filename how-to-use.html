How to Use

Welcome to the Smart Pantry system! This system is designed to help you keep track of your pantry items and reduce food waste. You can add new items to your inventory, view expiring items, and find recipes based on the items you have in your pantry.

Here's how you can use the system:

1. Add New Item:

Click on the "Add New Item" link in the sidebar to add a new item to your inventory. You can scan the barcode of the item or manually enter the barcode number. Once you have added the item, you can enter the expiry date and upload an image if you'd like.

2. Inventory:

Click on the "Inventory" link in the sidebar to view all the items in your inventory. You can see the barcode, product name, expiry date, and image of each item. You can also find recipes based on the items you have in your pantry.

3. Expiring Soon:

Click on the "Expiring Soon" button in the inventory tab to view all the items in your inventory that are expiring soon. You can see the barcode, product name, expiry date, and image of each item.

4. Find Recipes:

Click on the "Find Recipes with Selected Items" button to find recipes based on the items you have selected in your inventory. You can see the name of the recipe, ingredients, and instructions.

Water Usage Monitor/ Smart shower

Welcome to the Water Usage Monitor! This system is designed to help you keep track of your water usage and set goals to reduce water consumption. You can view your water usage over time, set a water goal, and reset your water usage.

Here's how you can use the system:

1. Water Usage Display:

The Water Usage Display shows you the total water usage and the last update time.

2. Set Water Goal:

You can set a water goal by entering the desired amount of water in litres and clicking the "Set Goal" button.

3. Progress Bar:

The Progress Bar shows you the progress towards your water goal.

4. Reset Button:

You can reset your water usage by clicking the "Reset Water Usage" button.

5. Water Usage Over Time:

The Water Usage Over Time graph shows you the water usage over the past 7 days

That's it! You are now ready to start using the Smart Pantry and Water Tracker system. Enjoy!
